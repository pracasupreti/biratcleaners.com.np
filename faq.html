Frequently Asked Questions

How can I schedule a cleaning?
Take a look at the 'Book Now' link and complete the form. The same working day, a confirmation email from our customer service will arrive, confirming the reservation. To schedule your cleaning service, you can also give +977-9852025735 a call.
What happens after I make a reservation?
An email confirming the services you reserved and the time of the appointment will be sent to you. Please check that all the information in this email is correct by reviewing it.
Is a Deep Clean Required?
For those who are new to us or haven't had a professional clean in a few months, we strongly suggest a Deep Clean. Visit our Services page for additional details about our Deep Clean.
Must I be at home when the cleaning is happening?
Well, you don't! Whether you choose to be at home for your appointment is entirely up to you. You can even just leave a key and we'll take care of everything if that's more your style!

When will I be charged?
When you book online or via phone, we ask for your credit card number to hold the reservation. However, you will not be charged until we have finished your cleaning service. If you sign up for recurring service, you will be automatically billed after each cleaning is complete.
Are hourly services available?
An hourly service might make sense in some situations. When we reserve hourly services, we have a minimum of four hours. This is usually saved for really big houses, houses where we are just cleaning a few rooms, or houses with a lot of dirt or clutter. Please be aware that our hourly service is not guaranteed, and if you use it, we are unable to return and reclean.
Does getting a cleaning cost extra since I've never had one before?
Even though we try our best to maintain all of our flat-rate pricing, there may be additional costs beyond the flat-rate fee for homes that are particularly dirty or larger than typical for the number of bedrooms and bathrooms specified.
How do you deal with cancellations?
When we clean your place, we never sign any contracts. All we ask is that you give us 48 hours' notice if you need to reschedule or cancel. There is a $70 fee if you need to cancel or reschedule within 48 hours. 50% of the cleaning fee is due if you cancel within three hours of the scheduled appointment. We will charge the full amount if you cancel with the cleaner there or if we are locked out.
