Privacy and Policy

Security:

Although we work hard to use commercially reasonable measures to protect your Personal Information, we cannot guarantee its complete security. We take great care to protect your Personal Information, but keep in mind that no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

Employee safety:

Because our cleaning technicians are the backbone of our company, we take great pride in ensuring their safety. The areas that need to be cleaned must be easily accessible, free of hazards, and have indoor temperatures that are suitable for their work.

No unethical enticement of workers:

We at Biratcleaners take great pride in our amazing staff members and spend a great deal of time, money, and energy finding, selecting, and developing the best candidates. Regretfully, a few former clients have attempted to "poach" our staff members in an attempt to profit from our diligence. Please be aware that unless Biratcleaners receives payment in the form of a $2,500 finder fee, employees are not permitted under the terms of their employment contract to accept private work for Biratcleaners clients. Kindly refrain from approaching our staff for direct hire or side work beyond the scope of this agreement. This is not only unfair to our small business, but it also puts our employee in an uncomfortable situation. All future services will end immediately if you try to hire an employee for private hire without paying the required finder fee.

Extra Services available with prior notification:

We provide a variety of Add-On services that can be scheduled in addition to your primary cleaning, in addition to our standard cleaning menus. Any cleaning can have these services added to it on an as-needed or ongoing basis. For pricing and scheduling, please contact us at info@biratcleaners.com.np or +977-9852025735. It is not possible to add on services on the day of the cleaning; instead, they must be requested at least one business day in advance of your scheduled cleaning.
